HIFU Body Fat Reduction & Tightening

Stubborn Fat Cells?

Even with rigorous exercise and intensive weight loss programs, stubborn fat cells can be challenging to eliminate.
High heat energy is produced by high-intensity focused ultrasound (HIFU), which kills the targeted fat. This can be done on the arms, inner thighs, bra fat, flanks, and double chin.
The Ultraformer III is a focused ultrasound device that sends concentrated ultrasonic energy into various layers of the skin and body without harming the skin's surface. It concentrates energy on adipose (fatty) tissue at a temperature of up to 75 degrees Celsius. This reduces Adipocytes (fat cells) and also aids in promoting collagen production to tighten the skin.
